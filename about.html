About The App

Shelton Pour

Cocktail Knowledge Platform

A modern bartending reference app.

Shelton Pour was built as a mobile-first cocktail library focused on speed, clarity, and usability. Instead of burying users in one endless scrolling page, the app separates core experiences into dedicated screens: drinks, search, builder, and recipes.

Included Features

  • Swipeable drink browsing
  • Ingredient-based cocktail builder
  • Recipe history and variations
  • Glassware and garnish specifications
  • Mobile-style navigation

Scalable Structure

The cocktail data is stored in data/cocktails.js, allowing the collection to scale from dozens of drinks to thousands.
